What data will be collected?
This extension collects basic, anonymous data about how many clicks you do per week collected.
Collection Data:
- TimeId: An hourly timestamp with the offset to GMT
- Week: The actual week of the year (week starts at Sunday)
- Year: The actual year
- Clicks: The clicks per hour
